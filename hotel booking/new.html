About Us

GK Hotel offers the best accommodations with top-notch services to ensure a memorable stay. Located in the heart of the city, we provide easy access to major attractions and business centers.

Check-in time: 2:00 pm
Check-out time: 12:00 pm

Set in the Express Mall complex, this modern hotel is 2.7 km from Marina Beach and 3.4 km from Chennai Central railway station. Featuring wood floors and colourful accents, the casual rooms also offer Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, minifridges, and tea and coffeemaking facilities, plus desks with ergonomic chairs. Suites add kitchenettes with microwaves, plus separate living rooms with sofas and dining tables. There's a bright vegetarian restaurant, along with an airy lobby lounge and 3 conference rooms. Breakfast is available.
